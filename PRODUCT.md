# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Primary Users (Customers)**: Vietnamese gamers and tech-savvy individuals who need to buy phone cards, game cards, and top-up services. They value speed, reliability, and competitive pricing (discounts).
- **Secondary Users (Admins/Employees)**: Business owners and staff managing products, categories, orders, and customer loyalty points. They need an efficient, high-density dashboard.

## Product Purpose
Votandan is a premium digital product marketplace and loyalty system. It aims to provide the most seamless card top-up experience in Vietnam, backed by a robust reward system (Loyalty Points) that encourages repeat purchases.

## Brand Personality
- **Futuristic**: State-of-the-art interface that feels ahead of its time.
- **High-Performance**: Snappy interactions, real-time updates, and zero friction.
- **Premium**: A "luxury tool" feel, even for common tasks like buying a card.
- **Trustworthy**: Secure, transparent, and authoritative.

## Anti-references
- **Generic Bootstrap/SaaS Templates**: Avoid the "white background, blue buttons" look.
- **Cluttered E-commerce Sites**: Avoid excessive banners, blinking text, and chaotic layouts.
- **2010s Admin Panels**: No standard gray tables and plain forms.

## Design Principles
- **Atmospheric Depth**: Use subtle gradients, blur, and layering to create a sense of space and focus.
- **Choreographed Feedback**: Every action should have a purposeful, smooth animation or transition.
- **Data as Art**: In the admin panel, information should be displayed beautifully, not just functionally.
- **Direct Action**: Minimize clicks for the primary task (buying a card).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG 2.1 Level AA**: Focus on high contrast (especially in dark mode) and keyboard navigability.
- **Responsive-First**: The customer experience must be flawless on mobile devices.
